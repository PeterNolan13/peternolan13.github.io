Background

I started programming my sophomore year at DePauw University while pursuing a minor in computer science. At DePauw I expanded my programming experience through Introduction to Computer Science, Data Structures, and Computer Systems. However, I had not become truly passionate about programming until the summer of 2017, when I attended a one month "Smart Launch Tech" program hosted by the Independent Colleges of Indiana and Eleven Fifty Academy. During this program, I received an industry recognized Gold Badge. In addition, I learned web development and utilized these skills in an assortment of projects, which are available below. I also gained knowledge of tech security, legal issues, compliance issues, an understanding of high-tech work environments and the wide range of careers in the tech industry. Since July of 2017, I have been a freelance web developer. Furthermore, I attended a two day class during the Smart Launch Tech program that provided valuable knowlege of Agile, Scrum, and Kanban methodologies. I plan on earning my scrum master certification later this year.

I recently completed a Web Security course at the University College Cork in Ireland, where I learned about: cryptography, access control, user authentication, database and cloud security, malicious software, DOS attacks, intrusion detection, firewalls and intrusion prevention systems. This semester I am taking Object Oriented Software Development.
