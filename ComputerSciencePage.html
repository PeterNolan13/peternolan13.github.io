Background

I started programming my sophomore year at DePauw University while pursuing a minor in computer science. At DePauw I expanded my programming experience through Introduction to Computer Science, Data Structures, and Computer Systems. However, I had not become truly passionate about programming until I attended the ICI/Eleven Fifty Academy Smart Launch Tech program the summer of 2017. During this program, I received an industry recognized Gold Badge. In addition, I learned web development and utilized these skills in an assortment of projects, which are available below. I also gained knowledge of tech security, legal issues, compliance issues, an understanding of high-tech work environments and the wide range of careers in the tech industry. Furthermore, I attended a two day class that provided valuable knowlege of Agile, Scrum, and Kanban methodologies.

I plan on taking several more computer science classes through DePauw University and getting my scrum master certification. In addition, I will continue teaching myself languages using SoloLearn and other online services. I have been a freelance web developer since July 2017 and I am currently taking a web security course at the University College Cork in Ireland.
